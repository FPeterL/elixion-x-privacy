Elixion X

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Elixion X ("the Game") is a mobile rhythm and tap game. This Privacy Policy explains how the Game handles user data.

1. Data Collection

Elixion X does not collect, store, or share personal data.

  • No account is required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are currently used.

2. Local Data Storage

The Game stores some information locally on your device only in order to function properly and save progress.

  • high scores
  • coins and unlocked cosmetic items
  • equipped skins and settings
  • beat mode related local progress or cached analysis

This data is not uploaded to a server and is not shared with the developer or third parties.

3. User-Provided Audio Files

In Beat Mode, players can choose and load their own audio files from their device. These files are processed locally on the device to generate gameplay content.

  • Audio files are not uploaded.
  • Audio files are not shared.
  • Audio analysis happens locally within the app.

Users are solely responsible for the audio files they choose to use within the Game. This includes ensuring they have the legal right to use any music or audio content.

Elixion X does not provide, distribute, or host any copyrighted audio content, and does not verify the ownership or licensing of user-provided files.

The Game acts only as a local tool for processing user-selected files and has no involvement in the acquisition of such content.

4. Advertising

Elixion X may include optional rewarded advertisements in the future. These ads, if added, will only be shown when the player chooses to watch them in exchange for an in-game reward.

If advertising is added later, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect the use of third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

5. In-App Purchases

Elixion X may offer optional in-app purchases for virtual currency or cosmetic content. Payment transactions are handled by the platform provider (such as Google Play), not by the Game directly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Elixion X is intended for users aged 13 and older. The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Because Elixion X does not operate a backend service and does not collect personal data, user information remains stored locally on the device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be posted on this page together with a new “Last updated” date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at:
